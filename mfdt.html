My friends do things: Chern makes stuff with wood

09 September 2020


I managed to get a pair of old rollerskates from an antique market in Melaka. It seemed like a fun idea to pay homage to skateboarding's history and turn these crusty skates into a janky rollerboard.

The rollerskates ended up in a bag in a corner of my room for a couple years before fate and a global pandemic brought my friend Chern back to Malaysia. He'd gotten into woodworking and is always down to do random projects, so we figured why not?


My friends do things


I started Subscript as an excuse to work on random creative projects, particularly the silly and financially pointless ones. I might extend this into a series that looks at some of the creative things my friends get up to, because they really do do wonderful things. Who knows if I'll get to episode 2, but I guess here's the first one: Chern makes stuff with wood.
